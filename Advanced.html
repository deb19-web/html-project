Advanced Home Workout Program

This program incorporates high-intensity exercises and complex movements to challenge strength, endurance, and flexibility. It’s designed for those who have a solid foundation and are looking to push their limits.

Warm-Up (10 minutes)

  1. High Knees - 1 minute
    2. Tips:

    • Intensity: Adjust weights and exercise difficulty to match your fitness level. Ensure you’re pushing yourself but not compromising form.

    • Form and Safety: Proper technique is crucial to prevent injury. Consider using a mirror or recording yourself to check form.

    • Progressive Overload: Gradually increase weights, reps, or sets as you become stronger.

    • Recovery: Allow at least one rest day between intense workouts to aid recovery. Incorporate light activities or stretching on rest days.

    This program can be performed 4-5 times a week, with adequate rest and recovery. Adjust the volume or intensity as needed to suit your individual fitness goals and conditions.

  2. Burpees - 1 minute
  3. Mountain Climbers - 1 minute
  4. Dynamic Lunges - 1 minute
  5. Arm Circles and Swings - 1 minute (30 seconds each direction)
  6. Leg Swings - 1 minute (30 seconds each leg)

Workout Routine (40-50 minutes)

  1. Front Squats with Dumbbells or Kettlebell
    • Reps: 12-15
    • Sets: 4
    • Rest: 60-90 seconds between sets
    • Equipment: Dumbbells or kettlebell (hold at shoulder level)
  2. Explosive Push-Ups
    • Reps: 10-12
    • Sets: 4
    • Rest: 60-90 seconds between sets
    • Variation: Push up with a clap or explosive push up from the ground
  3. Single-Leg Deadlifts
    • Reps: 10-12 per leg
    • Sets: 4
    • Rest: 60-90 seconds between sets
    • Equipment: Dumbbell or kettlebell
  4. Bulgarian Split Squats with Dumbbells
    • Reps: 12-15 per leg
    • Sets: 4
    • Rest: 60-90 seconds between sets
    • Equipment: Dumbbells or kettlebell
  5. Pistol Squats (to a box or chair if needed for balance)
    • Reps: 8-10 per leg
    • Sets: 3
    • Rest: 60-90 seconds between sets
  6. Renegade Rows
    • Reps: 12-15 per side
    • Sets: 4
    • Rest: 60-90 seconds between sets
    • Equipment: Dumbbells
  7. Plank to Push-Up
    • Reps: 10-15
    • Sets: 4
    • Rest: 60-90 seconds between sets
  8. Hanging Leg Raises or Lying Leg Raises
    • Reps: 15-20
    • Sets: 4
    • Rest: 60-90 seconds between sets
    • Equipment: Pull-up bar or lying on the floor
  9. Russian Twists with Medicine Ball or Dumbbell
    • Reps: 20-30 twists (10-15 each side)
    • Sets: 4
    • Rest: 60-90 seconds between sets
  10. Burpee Box Jumps
    • Reps: 10-15
    • Sets: 3
    • Rest: 60-90 seconds between sets
    • Equipment: Stable platform or box

Cool-Down (10 minutes)

  1. Standing Forward Bend - 1 minute
  2. Pigeon Pose - 1 minute per side
  3. Seated Hamstring Stretch - 1 minute per leg
  4. Cobra Stretch - 1 minute
  5. Child’s Pose - 1 minute
  6. Spinal Twist - 1 minute per side
