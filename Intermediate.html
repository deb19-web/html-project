Intermediate Home Workout Program

This program builds on basic exercises and incorporates more challenging movements to enhance strength, endurance, and flexibility. It requires minimal equipment, such as dumbbells or resistance bands, but can be adapted based on what you have available.

Warm-Up (5-10 minutes)

  1. Jump Rope (or simulate without a rope) - 1 minute
    2. Tips:

    • Proper Form: Ensure proper technique to avoid injury and maximize effectiveness.

    • Intensity: Adjust weights and reps as needed based on your fitness level and goals.

    • Progression: Increase weights, reps, or sets as you get stronger.

    • Recovery: Allow adequate rest and recovery between workouts to avoid overtraining.

    This program can be done 3-4 times a week. Incorporate rest days or lighter activity (like walking or yoga) to allow your body to recover.

  2. Dynamic Lunges - 1 minute (alternating legs)
  3. Arm Swings and Circles - 1 minute (30 seconds forward, 30 seconds backward)
  4. Leg Swings - 1 minute (30 seconds each leg)

Workout Routine (30-40 minutes)

  1. Goblet Squats
    • Reps: 12-15
    • Sets: 3
    • Rest: 60 seconds between sets
    • Equipment: Dumbbell or kettlebell (hold it at chest level)
  2. Push-Ups
    • Reps: 10-15
    • Sets: 3
    • Rest: 60 seconds between sets
    • Variation: Standard or decline (feet elevated)
  3. Dumbbell or Resistance Band Rows
    • Reps: 12-15
    • Sets: 3
    • Rest: 60 seconds between sets
    • Equipment: Dumbbell or resistance band
  4. Bulgarian Split Squats
    • Reps: 10-12 per leg
    • Sets: 3
    • Rest: 60 seconds between sets
    • Equipment: Elevated surface (like a chair) for rear leg
  5. Plank with Shoulder Taps
    • Reps: 20-30 taps (10-15 each side)
    • Sets: 3
    • Rest: 60 seconds between sets
  6. Dumbbell or Bodyweight Lunges
    • Reps: 12-15 per leg
    • Sets: 3
    • Rest: 60 seconds between sets
    • Equipment: Optional dumbbells
  7. 7. Russian Twists
    • Reps: 20-30 twists (10-15 each side)
    • Sets: 3
    • Rest: 60 seconds between sets
    • Equipment: Dumbbell or weight (optional)
  8. Glute Bridges with Single Leg Lift (for added challenge)
    • o Reps: 10-12 per leg
    • o Sets: 3
    • o Rest: 60 seconds between sets

Cool-Down (5-10 minutes)

  1. Standing Forward Bend - 1 minute
  2. Pigeon Pose - 1 minute per side
  3. Seated Hamstring Stretch - 1 minute per leg
  4. Chest Stretch - 1 minute (hands behind back, gently lift arms)
